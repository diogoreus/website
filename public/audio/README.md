# Audio Files for WinampPlayer

This directory should contain copyright-free audio tracks for the Flash era Winamp player.

## Expected Files

- `track1.mp3` - Electric Dreams
- `track2.mp3` - Pixel Perfect
- `track3.mp3` - Flash Forward

## Recommended Sources for Copyright-Free Music

### Free Music Archive (freemusicarchive.org)
- Look for punk/ska/rock genres
- Filter by Creative Commons licenses

### Audionautix (audionautix.com)
- All tracks are free with attribution
- Good for energetic, 2000s-style music

### Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music)
- Completely royalty-free
- No attribution required

### Incompetech (incompetech.com)
- Kevin MacLeod's music library
- Creative Commons Attribution

## Tony Hawk Soundtrack Vibe
For that authentic early 2000s Flash era feel, look for:
- Punk rock
- Ska-punk
- Alternative rock
- Energetic electronic

## Attribution
If using Creative Commons music, remember to add proper attribution in the site footer or a credits page.
